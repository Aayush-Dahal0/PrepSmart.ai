# PrepSmart.ai: Master Your Next Interview

PrepSmart.ai is an intelligent platform designed to help professionals ace their job interviews through AI-powered mock sessions. By simulating realistic interview scenarios and providing real-time feedback, PrepSmart.ai transforms the high-pressure preparation process into a structured path toward success.

##  Key Features

-   **AI Interviewer**: Conducts realistic interview sessions powered by Gemini AI.
-   **Domain-Specific Preparation**: Choose from dozens of industry domains (Software Engineering, Product Management, Finance, Legal, and more).
-   **Real-time Performance Metrics**: Receive immediate feedback and improvement tips during your session.
-   **Comprehensive Analytics**: Track progress over time with a modern dashboard and session history.
-   **Secure Authentication**: Robust account management using Supabase and Google OAuth.

##  Technical Stack

-   **Frontend**: React (Vite), TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, Shadcn UI.
-   **Backend**: FastAPI (Python), Google Gemini API.
-   **Infrastructure**: Supabase (Database/Auth), Vercel (Deployment).

## Engineering Challenges Solved

-   **Cross-Environment OAuth**: Synchronized authentication flows across multiple deployment environments (Preview vs. Production).
-   **SPA Routing in Subdirectories**: Implemented custom Vercel routing logic to handle deep-linking and page refreshes in a multi-directory project structure.
-   **Real-time Feedback Orchestration**: Managed asynchronous state transitions for low-latency AI responses.

## Impact

PrepSmart.ai provides a safe, objective environment for candidates to refine their communication, test their knowledge, and build the confidence necessary to land their dream job.
